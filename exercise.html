IMPORTANCE OF EXERCISE

Whatever your age, there's strong scientific evidence that being physically active can help you lead a healthier and happier life.Physical activity or exercise can improve your health and reduce the risk of developing several diseases like coronary heart disease, stroke, type 2 diabetes, cancer and cardiovascular disease. Physical activity and exercise can have immediate and long-term health benefits. Most importantly, regular activity can improve your quality of life.

  • More money in your wallet. ...

  • Lower risk of type 2 diabetes or diabetes complications. ...
  • Lower risk of some cancers. ...
  • Longer life. ...
  • Stronger bones and muscles.

      Swimming

      Benifits of swimming:-
      • being a relaxing and peaceful form of exercise.
      • alleviating stress.
      • improving coordination, balance and posture.
      • improving flexibility.
      • providing good low-impact therapy for some injuries and conditions.
      • providing a pleasant way to cool down on a hot day.

      Bicycling

      Benifits of bicycling:-
      • increased cardiovascular fitness.
      • increased muscle strength and flexibility.
      • improved joint mobility.
      • decreased stress levels.
      • improved posture and coordination.
      • strengthened bones.
      • decreased body fat levels.
      • prevention or management of disease.
